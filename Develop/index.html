About Me

Cherie Davis brings over a decade of marketing, communications, and public relations expertise, with rich and diverse experience across industries such as small business, services marketing, entertainment, and digital marketing agencies. Her broad background has fueled her passion for digital marketing, website development, and SEO.
She currently works in website development & SEO at The Rainmaker Institute, a full-service business development and digital marketing agency specializing in digital marketing and intake strategy for law firms.
She is also actively involved in her community and professional organizations, having served on the board of various organizations such as The American Cancer Society, The Junior League of Lexington, Kentucky Society of Marketing Professional Services (SMPS), EMERGE Lexington, and the National Association of Women in Construction (NAWIC)
